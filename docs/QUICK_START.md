# Quick Start Guide

Get connected to your TAK server in 5 minutes or less.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to the portal URL provided by your team
2. Click **"Create Account"**
3. Enter your:
   - Username
   - Email
   - Password
   - Callsign (your tactical identifier)
4. Click **"Register"**

---

## Step 2: Install the App

**Android Users:**
1. On the portal dashboard, tap the **ATAK icon**
2. Install from Google Play Store

**iPhone Users:**
1. On the portal dashboard, tap the **iTAK icon**
2. Install from the App Store

---

## Step 3: Connect to the Server

1. Find the large QR code on your dashboard under **"Login to ATAK"**
2. Open ATAK or iTAK on your phone
3. Go to Settings > Server Connections (ATAK) or Settings > Servers (iTAK)
4. Scan the QR code
5. Done! You're connected.

**Tip:** If you're viewing on your phone, just tap **"Open in ATAK"** instead of scanning.

---

## Step 4: Set Your Callsign (Optional)

If there's a **"Set Your Callsign"** section:
1. Scan that QR code with ATAK
2. Your callsign is now set to match your portal profile

---

## Step 5: Get Data Packages (If Available)

1. Look for **"Download Data Packages"** on your dashboard
2. Click **Download** for any packages you need
3. Import them into ATAK/iTAK

---

## Need Help?

- Check the full [User Guide](USER_GUIDE.md) for detailed instructions
- Contact your system administrator for account issues

---

**You're all set!** Open ATAK/iTAK and start collaborating with your team.
