## Getting Started

The OpenTAK Onboarding Portal is your one-stop solution for getting connected to your team's TAK (Team Awareness Kit) server. Whether you're using ATAK on Android or iTAK on iPhone, this portal makes setup quick and easy.

### What You'll Need

- A smartphone (Android for ATAK, iPhone for iTAK)
- The portal URL provided by your administrator
- Your login credentials (or ability to register)

---

## Creating Your Account

### Registration

1. Navigate to the portal URL provided by your administrator
2. Click **"Create Account"** on the login page
3. Fill in the required information:
   - **Username**: Choose a unique username
   - **Email**: Your email address (for password recovery)
   - **Password**: Create a strong password
   - **Callsign**: Your tactical callsign (this is what others will see on the map)
4. Click **"Register"**
5. Check your email for a verification link (if email verification is enabled)

### Logging In

1. Go to the portal URL
2. Enter your username and password
3. Click **"Login"**

### Forgot Password?

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Follow the link to create a new password

## Setting Up Your Profile

Your profile contains important information that identifies you on the TAK network.

### Accessing Your Profile

1. Log in to the portal
2. Click **"Edit Profile"** on your dashboard

### Profile Fields

- **Display Name**: Your full name or nickname
- **Callsign**: Your tactical identifier (shown on the map)
- **Email**: Your contact email address

### Updating Your Profile

1. Make your changes in the profile form
2. Click **"Save Changes"**

## Installing ATAK or iTAK

Before you can connect to the server, you need to install the ATAK or iTAK application.

### For Android Users (ATAK)

1. On your dashboard, look for the **ATAK icon**
2. Click the **"Android"** link or scan the QR code
3. Install ATAK from the Google Play Store
4. Open ATAK and complete the initial setup

### For iPhone Users (iTAK)

1. On your dashboard, look for the **iTAK icon**
2. Click the **"iPhone"** link or scan the QR code
3. Install iTAK from the App Store
4. Open iTAK and complete the initial setup

### Installing Meshtastic (Optional)

If your team uses Meshtastic for off-grid communications:

1. Look for the **Meshtastic icon** on your dashboard
2. Install the Meshtastic app for your platform
3. See the [Meshtastic Configuration](#meshtastic-configuration) section for setup

## Connecting to the Server

The portal provides QR codes that make connecting to the TAK server simple and secure.

### Using the ATAK QR Code (Android)

1. On your dashboard, find the **"Login to ATAK"** section
2. You'll see a large QR code with the ATAK logo
3. Open ATAK on your Android device
4. Go to **Settings > Network Preferences > Manage Server Connections**
5. Tap **"Import"** or **"Scan QR"**
6. Scan the QR code from your screen
7. The server connection will be automatically configured

**Alternatively**: Tap the **"Open in ATAK"** button if viewing on your Android device.

### Using the iTAK QR Code (iPhone)

1. On your dashboard, find the **"Login to ATAK"** section
2. Look for the iTAK QR code
3. Open iTAK on your iPhone
4. Go to **Settings > Servers**
5. Tap **"Scan QR Code"**
6. Scan the QR code from your screen

### QR Code Expiration

Your QR code may have an expiration time or usage limit:

- **Expires in**: Shows how long until the code expires
- **Uses**: Shows how many times the code has been used vs. maximum allowed

If your code expires, click **"Refresh Code"** to generate a new one.

## Setting Your Callsign

Your callsign is your tactical identifier that appears on the map when others see your location.

### Using the Callsign QR Code

1. If enabled, you'll see a **"Set Your Callsign"** section on your dashboard
2. Scan the QR code with ATAK
3. Your callsign will be automatically set to match your profile

### Manual Callsign Setup in ATAK

1. Open ATAK
2. Go to **Settings > Device Preferences**
3. Find **"Callsign"** and enter your assigned callsign

## Downloading Data Packages

Data packages contain maps, overlays, and other resources for your operations.

### Available Downloads

1. On your dashboard, find the **"Download Data Packages"** section
2. You'll see a table of available packages
3. Each package shows:
   - **Name**: Package identifier
   - **Description**: What the package contains

### Downloading a Package

1. Click the **"Download"** button next to the desired package
2. The file will download to your device
3. Import the package into ATAK/iTAK

### Importing in ATAK

1. Open ATAK
2. Go to **Tools > Data Package**
3. Navigate to your downloaded file
4. Tap to import

## Meshtastic Configuration

Meshtastic allows off-grid communication using LoRa radio modules.

### What is Meshtastic?

Meshtastic creates a mesh network for text messaging and location sharing without cellular or internet connectivity.

### Joining a Meshtastic Channel

1. On your dashboard, find the **"Meshtastic Configs"** section
2. Each configuration shows:
   - **Channel name**
   - **QR code** for easy setup
   - **Description** of the channel
3. Scan the QR code with the Meshtastic app
4. Or tap **"Open in Meshtastic"** if viewing on your phone

### Can't Scan the QR Code?

Click **"Copy Link"** to copy the channel URL, then paste it in the Meshtastic app.

## Viewing Your Radios

The portal tracks radios assigned to you by your administrator.

### Checking Your Assigned Radios

1. On your dashboard, find the **"Assigned Radios"** section
2. You'll see a list of radios with:
   - **Radio name**
   - **Platform/type**

If no radios are assigned, you'll see "No radios assigned."

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?

- Double-check your username and password
- Use **"Forgot Password?"** if you can't remember your password
- Contact your administrator if your account is locked

### QR Code Not Working?

- Make sure you're scanning with the correct app (ATAK for Android, iTAK for iPhone)
- Try clicking **"Refresh Code"** to generate a new one
- Check if the code has expired

### ATAK/iTAK Won't Connect to Server?

- Ensure you have an internet connection
- Verify the server is online (contact your administrator)
- Try re-scanning the QR code
- Check if your certificate has expired

### Meshtastic Channel Not Joining?

- Make sure you have a Meshtastic radio paired with your phone
- Verify the Meshtastic app is installed and open
- Try copying the link instead of scanning the QR code

### Data Package Won't Import?

- Ensure the file downloaded completely
- Check that you're importing in ATAK/iTAK (not just downloading)
- Contact your administrator if the package is corrupted

### Need More Help?

Contact your system administrator for:

- Account issues
- Access permissions
- Server connectivity problems
- Radio assignments
